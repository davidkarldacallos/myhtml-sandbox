Events Around The World

Event Name & Details Date Location
London International Mime Festival The wide-ranging schedule covers contemporary dance, puppetry, cinema, circus and lots of great children’s shows. 12 January-6 February Various London venues
Kiruna Snow Festival The highlight is a snow sculpture competition that attracts international talent and spawns genuinely amazing work, but there’s also the Snow Blower World Championships, figure skating and a kids’ playground – made from snow, naturally. 26-30 January Gruvvägen 1, 981 31 Kiruna, Sweden
Noć Muzeja (Museum Night) This popular annual event sees museums across Croatia open their doors for free on the last Friday in January, from 6pm-1am, with some hosting special exhibitions and events. 28 January Venues around Croatia
Yves Saint Laurent aux Musées To celebrate the 60th anniversary of Yves Saint Laurent’s first runway show, six major Parisian museums are staging a simultaneous exhibition to honour the iconic fashion designer. 29 January-15 May Six museums across Paris (Musée du Louvre, Musée d’Orsay, Centre Pompidou, Musée d’Art Moderne de Paris, Musée National Picasso-Paris, Musée Yves Saint Laurent Paris)
Armagh Fusion Festival If you’ve never visited Armagh before, there couldn’t be a better occasion than this culture-crammed celebration of the region’s history and heritage – if you have been before, there’s still plenty to learn. 4-20 February Venues around Armagh city
